About EDM VERSEX

EDM VERSEX is an independent online hub dedicated to electronic dance music. The focus stays on styles that live in clubs and festivals: house, trance, techno and neighbouring sounds that people actually move to.

The page exists to make following this world easier. Instead of sending you in many different directions, it brings together key elements of the scene in one place: club-ready tracks, recent arrivals, news from trusted music outlets and longer editorial notes.

Every part of the layout is designed with mobile use in mind. On a phone screen you should be able to scroll through the entire experience vertically, get a feel for what is happening and then decide which tracks or stories to spend more time with.

EDM VERSEX does not represent any single label or event. It is shaped by a genuine interest in how electronic music keeps evolving and how communities around it stay creative.
