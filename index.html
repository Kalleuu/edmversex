Listening guide & site experience

How to use the charts

EDM VERSEX gives you a clean overview of what is happening in global club sound. Follow the Top 50 list when you want to feel what is shaping dancefloors right now and switch to the Latest 20 feed when you want to catch fresh uploads before they spread.

The focus filter above the chart makes it easy to quickly highlight specific moods such as house, trance or techno. This way you can narrow the view without losing sight of the full picture.

Ads and user experience

Advertising on EDM VERSEX appears only inside rich content sections such as the header and long-form panels. There are no ads on empty utility views or purely functional pages, so the focus stays on music.

Analytics is used to understand which sections visitors engage with the most and to refine the page layout over time. The goal is to keep the experience immersive and fast while still providing clear opportunities for brands to reach an EDM audience.

Use the chat bubble in the corner to ask questions about EDM genres, track discovery and how to read the charts. The assistant does not control ads or account settings â€“ it is purely here to help you navigate music.

About EDM VERSEX

EDM VERSEX is designed as a high-quality, content-driven home for electronic dance music fans and DJs. The site combines automatically updated SoundCloud data with human-written explanations so that visitors can understand what they are seeing, not just scroll lists of track names.

Only public SoundCloud data is used. All API credentials live on a secure backend, and the site respects SoundCloud rate limits by caching chart data in short time windows. This keeps the experience fluid while avoiding unnecessary traffic.

If you work with labels, mixes or events, EDM VERSEX can serve as a landing page to showcase your sound while remaining transparent, policy-compliant and easy to use on both mobile and desktop devices.
