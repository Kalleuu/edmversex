Ads & experience

Display ads appear only inside rich content views such as the header area and long-form sections, never on empty or purely navigational screens. This layout respects AdSense policies on publisher content and ad placement.

Analytics is used to understand which sections visitors prefer and to optimise the order of content blocks for a smoother, more immersive experience.

About EDM VERSEX

EDM VERSEX is designed as an independent, content-rich outlet for electronic dance music fans and producers. It uses live SoundCloud data combined with written commentary to highlight tracks, artists and moods.

All SoundCloud API credentials are stored securely on the server side using Cloudflare Workers secrets, never inside the public HTML or JavaScript. This allows the charts to stay fresh while keeping your account safe.

If you run labels, podcasts or events, this page can also act as a central landing page where new listeners discover your sound and advertisers reach a focused audience of EDM fans.
