Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 November 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how EDM VERSEX (“we”, “us”, “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit https://edmversex.com.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that your browser stores on your device. They allow websites to remember information about your visit, keep you signed in, measure performance and show relevant content and advertising.

2. Types of cookies we use

We use different categories of cookies and similar technologies on EDM VERSEX:

  • Essential cookies These cookies are required for the site to function correctly and securely. They enable core features such as page navigation and basic security checks. The site cannot function properly without these cookies.
  • Preference cookies These cookies remember your choices and settings, for example language preferences or cookie consent choices, so you do not have to set them again on each visit.
  • Analytics cookies These cookies help us understand how visitors use the site, which pages are most popular and where we can improve the user experience. Data is usually aggregated and does not directly identify you.
  • Advertising cookies We use advertising cookies through partners such as Google AdSense. These cookies may be used to show ads that are more relevant to your interests and to measure ad performance. They can build a limited profile of your interests based on your browsing activity.

3. Third party cookies

Some cookies on this site are set by third parties. This includes:

  • Google AdSense for displaying and measuring advertising.
  • Embedded media or external platforms such as streaming services or social platforms when we embed players or external content. These services may set their own cookies in line with their privacy and cookie policies.

You can find more information about how Google uses cookies and advertising identifiers at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies .

4. Cookie consent and control

Where required by law, we ask for your consent before placing non essential cookies such as analytics and advertising cookies. You can accept or decline these cookies using the cookie banner or settings provided on the site.

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Delete cookies from your device.
  • Block cookies from certain sites.
  • Block third party cookies.
  • Receive notifications before a cookie is stored.

Please note that blocking certain cookies may impact site functionality or the relevance of ads and content.

5. Retention

Cookie lifetimes vary. Some cookies remain only for the duration of your session while others may stay on your device for longer or until you delete them. Exact retention periods depend on the specific cookie and its purpose.

6. Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when our use of cookies changes or when legal requirements change. The latest version is always available on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
