Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how EDM VERSEX (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses and protects personal data when you visit https://edmversex.com or interact with our content and advertisements.

1. Data we collect

We collect limited information that is necessary to operate and improve the site and to display advertising. This includes:

  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, approximate location based on IP, referral URLs and time stamps recorded in server logs.
  • Usage data such as pages viewed, clicks on links or content, time spent on pages and interactions with our music and news feeds.
  • Advertising data such as ad impressions and clicks that are processed by our advertising partners including Google AdSense.
  • Optional contact data when you choose to contact us by email or sign up for our newsletter. This may include your email address and any information you choose to share in your message.

2. Cookies and similar technologies

EDM VERSEX uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, measure traffic and show relevant advertising. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies required for basic site functionality and security.
  • Analytics and performance cookies that help us understand how visitors use the site and improve content and user experience.
  • Advertising cookies set by our partners such as Google AdSense to deliver personalised or contextual ads and to measure ad performance.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings and, where required by law, through the consent options shown on the site. For more information see our separate Cookie Policy.

3. Google AdSense and advertising

We display ads provided by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalised ads based on your previous interactions with websites. This may include the use of the Google advertising cookie and other identifiers.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from sites that use its services and how to manage your advertising settings at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads .

4. Legal bases for processing

Where applicable under European data protection law, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests for operating and securing the site, preventing abuse and improving our services.
  • Consent for placing certain cookies and for personalising advertising when required by law. You can withdraw consent at any time through your cookie settings or browser tools.

5. Data sharing and third parties

We share data with third parties only where necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. This may include:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers that store and process data on our behalf.
  • Analytics and advertising partners such as Google that process data in line with their own privacy policies.
  • Authorities or legal advisors where required by law or necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Log files and analytics data are retained for a limited period, after which they are deleted or anonymised where possible.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct or delete data, restrict processing or object to certain processing activities.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us. We may need to verify your identity before responding to your request.

8. International transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries outside your country of residence. Where required, we take appropriate measures to protect your data in line with applicable law.

9. Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when our services or legal requirements change. The latest version is always available on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
